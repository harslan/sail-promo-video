## CRITICAL CREATIVE DIRECTION

1. **This is NOT a corporate video.** No stock imagery vibes. No "diverse group of students smiling at laptops." Pure typography, motion, and emotion.

2. **The pacing should breathe.** Silence and space are as important as content. Let the big moments land. Don't fill every frame.

3. **Scene 6 is the climax.** Everything builds to "Ownership cannot." Treat it like the drop in a song. Everything before is the build-up.

4. **The L pillar is special.** In Scene 3, the other three pillars ask questions ("How do we connect?", "How does it work?", "What's missing or wrong?"). The L pillar makes a DECLARATION: "I will own what happens." This should feel visually and tonally different â€” bolder, more personal, warmer.

5. **The ethical moment in Scene 4 is the emotional proof.** Students raised fairness concerns UNPROMPTED. The word "â€” unprompted" should hit like a revelation.
